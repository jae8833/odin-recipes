Dakgalbi

Picture of Dakgalbi

Essentially, dak galbi / dakgalbi (닭갈비) is spicy chicken stir fried in a large pan, which is often a cast iron pan.

Ingredients
  • 500g /1.1 pounds chicken thigh fillets
  • 1/2 medium sweet potato (180g/6.3 ounces)
  • 1/2 small carrot (60g/2.1 ounces), diagonally sliced
  • 1/4 small cabbage (320g/0.7 pounds), shredded
  • 10 Korean perilla leaves (35g/1.2 ounces), thinly sliced
  • 18 fresh Korean rice cakes pieces (175g/ 6.1 ounces)
  • Some cooking oil (2 to 3 Tbsp) – I used rice bran oil
Steps
  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients in a bowl and mix them well.
  2. Pour the sauce over the chopped chicken and marinate for at least 30 mins.
  3. Preheat a large skillet on medium high heat and once heated add some cooking oil.
  4. Put all the vegetables and rice cakes into the skillet and add the meat on top. Cook them on medium high heat for 3-4 mins.
  5. Then reduce the heat to medium to medium low and cook further until all is cooked (about 10 - 15 mins).
  6. During cooking, stir often to avoid food sticking onto the pan.
    7. You can cover with a lid to speed up the cooking process.
